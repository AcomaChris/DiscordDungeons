# Player's Guide

Welcome to DiscordDungeons! This is a 3/4 view tile-based RPG you can play directly inside Discord or in your browser.

## Getting Started

Launch the game at [discorddungeons.com](https://discorddungeons.com). You can log in with your Discord account or play as a guest.

1. **Discord Login** — Authenticates via Discord OAuth2 for persistent identity
2. **Guest Login** — Enter a custom name and start playing immediately
